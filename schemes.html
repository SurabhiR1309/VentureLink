Government Schemes for Startups

Explore initiatives by the Indian Government to support and uplift new entrepreneurs

Key Government Schemes

Startup India

A flagship initiative offering tax benefits, funding support, and ease of compliance to boost entrepreneurship in India.

MUDRA Yojana

Provides microfinance loans up to ₹10 lakh to small entrepreneurs under Shishu, Kishore, and Tarun categories.

Atal Innovation Mission (AIM)

Promotes innovation through Atal Incubation Centres, fostering startups with mentoring and financial support.

Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE)

Offers collateral-free credit to new and existing micro & small enterprises.

Support for International Patent Protection in Electronics & IT (SIP-EIT)

Reimburses costs incurred for patent filing in foreign countries by Indian startups in electronics and IT sectors.

Stand-Up India

Supports women and SC/ST entrepreneurs with bank loans between ₹10 lakh and ₹1 crore for greenfield enterprises.
