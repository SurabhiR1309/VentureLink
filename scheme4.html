Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE)

About the Scheme

Established in 2000 as a joint initiative by the Government of India and SIDBI, the Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE) aims to facilitate easy, collateral-free credit access for India's Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs). By providing credit guarantees to lending institutions, CGTMSE significantly reduces the risk associated with unsecured loans, thereby encouraging wider credit flow to the sector.

Over its 25-year journey, CGTMSE has emerged as a critical enabler of MSME growth. In FY 2024–25, the scheme achieved a major milestone by approving guarantees worth ₹3 lakh crore, demonstrating its growing impact in bridging the credit gap for small businesses across India.

Key Features of CGTMSE:

  • Collateral-free loans of up to ₹5 crore for eligible MSEs.
  • Guarantee cover up to 85% of the loan amount, depending on the borrower profile.
  • Available for both new and existing MSEs engaged in manufacturing, services, and retail (including traders).
  • Encourages banks, NBFCs, and other financial institutions to lend confidently to MSMEs.
