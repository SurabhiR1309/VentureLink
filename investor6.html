Investor Profile

Detailed information about the investor

Nidhi Parmar

Location: Jaipur, India

Nidhi Parmar is the Managing Partner at EcoBridge Ventures, a mission-led investment firm focused on supporting environment-first business models. A passionate advocate for sustainability and green entrepreneurship, Nidhi plays a leading role in backing eco-conscious ventures that address environmental challenges through scalable solutions. She is known for identifying early-stage green innovators and helping them bring sustainable alternatives to mainstream markets.

Interest Areas

  • Social Impact Retail

  • Sustainable Logistics and Packaging
  • Youth-Led Climate Action
