Startup Name: AgroLink

AgroLink connects small farmers with agronomists and market experts using a mobile-first advisory platform.

Our Vision

AgroLink is a mobile-first agri-tech platform designed to empower small farmers by connecting them with agronomists and market experts. With features like AI-based crop diagnosis, a digital marketplace for selling produce, multilingual content, and 24/7 chatbot support, AgroLink ensures farmers receive timely, accessible, and personalized agricultural guidance.


Key Features


  • AI-based crop diagnosis
  • Digital marketplace for produce
  • Multilingual content
  • 24/7 chatbot support
