Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY)

About the Scheme

Launched in 2015, PMMY offers loans up to ₹10 Lakhs to small and micro enterprises engaged in manufacturing, trading, or services. The loans are provided through banks, NBFCs, and MFIs under three categories:

  • Shishu: Loans up to ₹50,000
  • Kishore: Loans from ₹50,001 to ₹5 Lakhs
  • Tarun: Loans from ₹5 Lakhs to ₹10 Lakhs

Eligibility:

All Indian citizens with a viable business plan are eligible. No collateral is required.

Where to Apply:

You can apply through commercial banks, regional rural banks, or MFIs.
