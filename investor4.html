Investor Profile

Detailed information about the investor

Kavita Rao

Location: Pune, India

Kavita Rao is the Director of Bright Future Foundation, a non-profit organization committed to transforming education and empowerment for children in underserved communities. With a background in education policy and grassroots advocacy, Kavita has been instrumental in nurturing initiatives that provide mentorship, access, and quality learning to students in need. Her investments focus on long-term educational upliftment and nurturing young potential from the ground up.

Interest Areas

  • Educational Equity
  • Student Mentorship Programs
  • Academic Access & Support
  • Early Childhood Learning
  • Digital Tools for Low-Income Classrooms

Investments Made

EduMentor

Allocated ₹8,00,000 in funding to support a grassroots mentorship initiative that offers academic guidance and personal development support to school students in underprivileged areas.

ClassConnect

₹7,25,000 invested in a blended learning platform offering live sessions and academic counseling for students in government schools.

InspireEd

₹6,00,000 provided to support a mentorship program connecting college volunteers with secondary school students in slum communities.

VisionShala

₹5,50,000 seed funding for an after-school center offering tutoring, career awareness, and life skills workshops in low-income neighborhoods.

UdaanPathshala

₹9,00,000 contributed toward setting up mobile education vans delivering mentorship and subject-specific guidance to students in remote villages.
