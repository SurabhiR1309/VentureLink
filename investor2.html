Charmi Chavda

Location: Ahmedabad, India

Charmi Chavda is the CEO and Managing Director of Rchitrix Digital Solution, a forward-thinking tech and media firm focused on empowering digital-first initiatives led by youth and grassroots innovators. With a strong background in digital transformation and content ecosystems, Charmi is known for supporting student-led ventures that create social impact through knowledge-sharing and online platforms. Her mission is to amplify informed voices that can drive real societal change.
