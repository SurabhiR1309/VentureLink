Startup India

About the Scheme

Launched on January 16, 2016, by the Government of India, Startup India aims to foster innovation, support entrepreneurship, and create a robust startup ecosystem. The initiative facilitates fast-tracking of regulatory approvals, offers tax exemptions, funding support, and simplifies compliance procedures to empower early-stage companies.

As of December 31, 2024, over 157,700 startups have been recognized by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT)—a remarkable leap from just 502 at inception. These startups have collectively created more than 1.728 million direct jobs, significantly contributing to sectors such as:

  • IT Services: 210,000 jobs
  • Healthcare and Lifesciences: 151,000 jobs
  • Professional & Commercial Services

Eligibility Criteria for DPIIT Recognition:

All Indian citizens with a viable business plan are eligible. No collateral is required.

Where to Apply:

  • Must be a private limited company, registered partnership firm, or LLP.
  • Should be less than 10 years old from the date of incorporation.
  • Annual turnover should not exceed INR 100 crore in any financial year.
  • Should be working towards innovation, development, or improvement of products/services or scalable business models.
  • Must not have been formed by splitting or reconstructing an existing business.
