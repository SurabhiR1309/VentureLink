Startup Name: Med4All

Med4All is a social-healthcare startup targeting rural India’s healthcare access gap.

Featuring Items and Our Vision

Med4All is a social-healthcare startup dedicated to bridging the healthcare access gap in rural India. Through its innovative app, Med4All integrates telemedicine, doorstep medicine delivery, and digital health record-keeping to provide accessible and affordable healthcare for underserved communities.


Key Features


  • WhatsApp-integrated teleconsultation
  • Virtual monthly health camps
  • NGO partnerships for free/subsidized medicine delivery
  • Digital health record management
  • Awareness content in local languages
