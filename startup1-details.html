Startup Name: PramukhamArt

A passionate housewife turned artist, she founded PramukhamArt to showcase her heartfelt, handcrafted creations.

Featuring Items and Our Vision

  • A dynamic platform celebrating the richness of artistic expression through a vibrant collection of traditional and contemporary art forms. Our startup is dedicated to showcasing unique paintings, intricate designs, and handcrafted works that embody cultural heritage and modern creativity.
  • At the heart of our mission is the belief that art is a universal language—each piece in our gallery tells a story of skill, passion, and identity. From bold abstract expressions to delicate folk traditions, our curated collections invite art lovers, collectors, and creators to explore and connect.
  • Our platform not only serves as a visual gallery but also as a hub for meaningful engagement. Visitors can inquire about purchasing or commissioning custom artworks tailored to their tastes and ideas. We welcome collaborations and conversations, making art more accessible and interactive.
  • Blending timeless techniques with contemporary flair, PramukhamArt is more than a gallery—it's a journey through culture, creativity, and collaboration. Join us as we bring majestic art forms to the forefront of the digital space.
