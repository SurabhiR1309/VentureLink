Support for International Patent Protection in Electronics & IT (SIP-EIT)

About the Scheme

The Support to International Patent Protection in Electronics & IT (SIP-EIT) scheme, spearheaded by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), aims to empower Indian tech startups and SMEs in protecting their innovations on a global scale. By offering financial assistance for international patent filing, the scheme reduces cost-related barriers and encourages broader IP ownership in strategic sectors.

Under SIP-EIT, eligible enterprises can receive reimbursement of up to 50% of total patent filing expenses, with a ceiling of ₹15 lakh per invention. This support operates on a matching-basis model, ensuring co-investment by the applicant and promoting financial responsibility while advancing innovation.


Key Highlights of SIP-EIT:

  • Applicable to technology startups, MSMEs, and recognized R&D institutions.
  • Covers expenses related to filing, attorney fees, examination, and processing of international patents.
  • Encourages global competitiveness by safeguarding indigenous innovations abroad.
  • Promotes IP awareness and innovation-led growth in the electronics and IT sectors.
