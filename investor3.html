Investor Profile

Detailed information about the investor

Suresh Iyer

Location: Bengaluru, India

Suresh Iyer is a seasoned art curator and investor, currently associated with CultureHub India, an initiative focused on preserving and promoting indigenous art and cultural entrepreneurship. With over two decades of experience in India’s creative industries, Suresh bridges the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern platforms. He is known for empowering grassroots artists and student creators by investing in scalable ventures that celebrate artistic expression and cultural heritage.

Interest Areas

  • Artisan Empowerment
  • Creative Economy Startups
  • E-Commerce for Local Art
  • Cultural Education & Workshops
