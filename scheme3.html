Atal Innovation Mission (AIM)

About the Scheme

Launched in 2016 under the aegis of NITI Aayog, the Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) is a flagship initiative to promote a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship across India. AIM fosters innovation at both the school and startup levels through strategic programs like Atal Tinkering Labs (ATLs) and Atal Incubation Centres (AICs).

As of 2023, AIM has established over 10,000 Atal Tinkering Labs in schools across the country. These labs provide more than 2 million students with hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies such as 3D printing, robotics, and electronics, empowering them to become creators and problem-solvers from a young age.

In parallel, AIM has also nurtured the startup ecosystem through Atal Incubation Centres. By January 2025, approximately 3,500 startups have been incubated across 72 AICs, receiving mentoring, funding access, and infrastructure support vital for early-stage entrepreneurial growth.

Key Components of AIM:

  • Atal Tinkering Labs (ATLs):Innovation spaces in schools to encourage STEM learning.
  • Atal Incubation Centres (AICs) Incubators offering support to promising startups and entrepreneurs.
  • Atal New India Challenges & ARISE: Programs to promote product and process innovations in partnership with industry and government.
