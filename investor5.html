Detailed information about the investor

Arjun Patel

Location: Hyderabad, India

Arjun Patel is the Chief Operating Officer at WellnessTech Pvt. Ltd., a forward-looking company dedicated to promoting digital health innovation across India. With a background in sports science and tech entrepreneurship, Arjun brings a unique lens to the fitness-tech space. He is passionate about supporting scalable wellness platforms that combine personalized fitness with technology, making healthier lifestyles more accessible to urban and semi-urban populations alike.
