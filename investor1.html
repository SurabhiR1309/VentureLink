Investor Profile

Detailed information about the investor

Aditya Desai

Location: Mumbai, India

Aditya Desai is the Co-Founder of HealNow Foundation, a philanthropic organization committed to advancing accessible mental healthcare solutions in India. With a passion for youth well-being and emotional resilience, Aditya focuses on funding and supporting early initiatives that bridge the gap in mental health services for underserved communities. His recent involvement includes supporting platforms aimed at student wellness and emotional outreach.

Interest Areas

  • Mental Health Advocacy
  • Student Wellness Platforms
  • Digital Therapy Solutions
  • Emotional Support Infrastructure

Investments Made

MindSpace

Allocated ₹11,000 in seed funding to support a mental health awareness community offering virtual counseling, helplines, and wellness resources for students.

TalkEasy

₹30,000 invested in a mobile app providing AI-powered emotional check-ins and instant access to certified counselors for teens and college students.

PeerLift

₹18,000 granted to support a peer support training program that enables students in Tier 2 colleges to assist peers with emotional first-aid and mental well-being.
